About The Event

Hi there Final Fantasy TCG players! We're glad to announce we'll be hosting the Benelux Reraise event in Leuven (Belgium). You can find all the information in the categories below. We hope to welcome you all in October!

Event Schedule

Day 1 will consist of Best-Of-One rounds. The amount of rounds will depend on the amount of participators. You can play one our of your 2 decks during the first day.

Day 2, the top 16 will battle to become the Reraise Benelux champion in a Best-Of-Three 2-deck format. If a card is present in one deck, no copies may be played in the other.

For those who didn't make it to the top 16, side events will be organised with some extra prize support. Format will depend on the interest of the players.

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

Ontmoetingscentrum De Kring
Jozef Pierrestraat 60
3010 Leuven
Belgium

The event will be on the first floor. Sadly the event hall doesn't have an elevator and only has stairs available to get to the first floor. We are really sorry for this inconvenience.

The event hall is on a 10 minute walk from the train station in Leuven.

Food & Drinks

In the event hall, we will provide a variety of food and drinks at very cheap prices.

Drinks: Water (flat and sprinkling), Coke, Coke Zero, Limonade, Stella Artois, ...

Food: Sandwiches (vegetables, cheese, ham and other varieties) & hotdogs

Snacks: Belgian chocolate, candy, cookies, ...

Parking facilities

The area around the event hall, has a limited amount of parking spots available. If you want to park there, you're required to use a parking disc between 9:00 and 18:00 from Monday until Saturday. On sunday you can park for free.

An easy alternative is to park your car near the train station (address: IJzerenwegstraat, 3010 Leuven Belgium). You can park your car over there for 20 euro per day and it's right next to our nearby hotels.
