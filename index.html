Event Schedule

Day 1 will consist of Best-Of-One rounds. The amount of rounds will depend on the amount of participators.
You are required to play 2 decks. If a card is present in one deck, no copies may be played in the other. The first half of the rounds, you'll play your first deck. The second half, your second deck. Please clearly mention on your decklists which deck is "DECK 1" and which is "DECK 2".

Day 2, the top 16 will battle to become the Reraise Benelux champion in a Best-Of-Three 2-deck format. Top 16 players can choose which deck they want to start with.

For those who didn't make it to the top 16, side events will be organised with some extra prize support. Format will depend on the interest of the players.

Food & Drinks

In the event hall, we will provide a variety of food and drinks at economical prices.

Drinks: Water (still and sparkling), Coke, Coke Zero, Lemonade, Stella Artois, Leffe Blond/Brown, ...

Food: Sandwiches (cheese, ham and other varieties) & hotdogs

Snacks: Belgian chocolate, candy, cookies, ...

Entry fees for day 1 and day 2 side events can be payed by card. Also merchandise can be payed by card. Since catering is provided by the event venue, we will only be able to accept cash for that, so please bring some cash! (we only accept Euro)

Travelling

By car

The area around the event hall, has a limited amount of parking spots available. If you want to park there, you're required to use a parking disc between 9:00 and 18:00 from Monday until Saturday. On sunday you can park for free.

An easy alternative is to park your car near the train station (address: IJzerenwegstraat, 3010 Leuven Belgium). You can park your car over there for €20,00 a day and it's right next to our nearby hotels.


By train

The event hall is near the train station in Leuven (Belgium). Also our nearest hotels are only a 100 meter walk form the station.

By plane

The easiest way to get there, is by combining a flight to Brussels Airport in Zaventem and taking the train from Zaventem to Leuven afterwards.
The train from Zaventem to Leuven only takes 13 minutes and you'll arrive right next to our nearest hotels and very close to the event venue.

F.A.Q

  • Will there be enough entry seats?

    Absolutely! We are able to welcome 120 players, so there so should be more than enough space for everyone!

  • Are there any rules regarding Covid-19?

    Currently, Belgium doesn't have any specific rules regarding Covid-19. Although we advice to keep everything safe. Wash your hands regularly and we'll try to have everyone seated on a safe distance from each other.

  • Where can we eat or drink before & after the event?

    If you like cocktails, "Entreprise" is one of the best bars for that!

    Are you looking for some typical Belgian food? We would advice the following 4 places. Good Belgian food at decent prices! mykene Troubadour Kosmopol Van De Weyer
